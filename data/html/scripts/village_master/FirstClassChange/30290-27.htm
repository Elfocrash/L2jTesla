Grand Master Xenos:
To become a Shillien Oracle, the appropriate level and token are required. Your level must be at least 20 to qualify to change profession. Also, a token that proves that you passed the Test of the Shillien Oracle is required.
However, it seems your level is not high enough, and you have not yet passed the Test of the Shillien Oracle. First, try to raise your level to 20 or higher. To take the test, you should visit Sorceress Sidra of the Dark Elven Guild in the town of Gludio. You can change profession to Shillien Oracle only if you pass her test and bring back the orb of abyss as a token. You can get to the town of Gludio by following the road towards the east.
