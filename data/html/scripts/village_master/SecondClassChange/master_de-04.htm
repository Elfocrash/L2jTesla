Each mark requires its own test.
The Mark of Duty is reserved for those who pass the Trial of Duty. You must be above level 35 to qualify for this test. To receive the Mark of Duty, go to Giran Castle Town and meet Grand Master Hannavalt.
The Mark of Fate is given to those who pass the Testimony of Fate. You must be above level 37 to take this test. To take the Testimony of Fate, go to Giran Castle Town and meet Dark Elven Magister Kaira.
The Mark of Witchcraft is reserved for those who pass the Test of Witchcraft. You must be above level 39 to take the test. To take this test, go to the Death Pass and visit Orim of the Shadow.
