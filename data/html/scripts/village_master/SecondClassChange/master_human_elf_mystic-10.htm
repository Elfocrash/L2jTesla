A Sorcerer is an advanced form of a Wizard. Uses elemental magic for long-range offensive attack. Strong offensive power but lacks stamina. Works best with a party. Very valuable during melee and siege. With groups of other sorcerers becomes a most fearsome battlefield weapon.
To become a Sorcerer your level must be over 40 and you must collect the Mark of the Scholar, the Mark of Trust, and the Mark of the Magus.
