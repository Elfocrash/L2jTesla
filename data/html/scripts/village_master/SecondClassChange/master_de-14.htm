The Abyss Walker is quite similar to the Human Treasure Hunter, but stronger. The Abyss Walker is very useful to its party. They can act as an Assassin during Siege Battle, and inflict strong damage upon opponents.
They have basic skills such as strengthening light equipment and creeping up on enemies and striking deadly blows. They are also quite adept at dodging first attacks by monsters.
To be a Abyss Walker your level must be over 40 and you must collect the Mark of the Seeker, the Mark of Fate, and the Mark of the Searcher.
