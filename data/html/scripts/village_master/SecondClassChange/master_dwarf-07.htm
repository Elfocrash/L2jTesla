A Warsmith's mission is to create weapons of war. He is also a superb Summoner, having the strength to fight at the front alongside the golems he summons during war. Warsmiths are also the only Summoners capable of conjuring siege weapons.
To advance to Warsmith your level must be over 40 and you must possess the Mark of the Guildsman, the Mark of Prosperity, and the Mark of the Maestro.
