Each mark requires its own test.
The Mark of the Seeker is reserved for those who pass the Trial of the Seeker. You must be above level 35 to qualify. To take this test you must visit Master Dufner of Giran Castle Town.
The Mark of Fate is reserved for those who pass the Testimony of Fate. You must be above level 37 to qualify. To take this test, go to Giran Castle Town and meet Dark Elven Magister Kaira.
And finally, the Mark of the Searcher is only given to those who pass the Test of the Searcher. You must be above level 39 to qualify. To take this test, visit Master Luther at the Hunters Village.
