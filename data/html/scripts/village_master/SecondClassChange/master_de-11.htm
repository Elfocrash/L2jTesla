Each mark requires its own test.
The Mark of the Pilgrim is only given to those who pass the Trial of the Pilgrim. You must be above level 35 to qualify. To take this trial you must visit the Hermit Santiago in the Estate of Gludio. He lives at a farm with Piotur.
The Mark of Fate is reserved for those who pass the Testimony of Fate. You must be above level 37 to qualify. To take the Testimony of Fate you must go to Giran Castle Town and meet Dark Elven Magister Kaira.
And last, the Mark of the Reformer is reserved for those who pass the Trial of the Reformer. You must be above level 39 to qualify. To take the Trial of the Reformer, visit Priestess Pupina at the temple of Giran Castle Town.
