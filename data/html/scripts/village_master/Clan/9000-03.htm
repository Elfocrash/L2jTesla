[Clan level raise informations]
The clan level can be raised. Raising clan level requires the following:
Level 1 - SP : 30,000, Adena : 650,000 Level 2 - SP : 150,000, Adena : 2,500,000 Level 3 - SP : 500,000, Item : Blood Mark Level 4 - SP : 1,400,000, Item : Alliance Manifesto Level 5 - SP : 3,500,000, Item : Seal of Aspiration Level 6 - Reputation : 10,000, Members : More than 30 Level 7 - Reputation : 20,000, Members : More than 80 Level 8 - Reputation : 40,000, Members : More than 120
[Hints]
Get the Blood Mark by hunting the Blood Queen in Dragon Valley.
To get the Aliance Manifesto, see Sir Kristof Rodemai in the Town of Giran.
To get the Seal of Aspiration, see Sir Gustav Athebaldt in the Town of Oren
