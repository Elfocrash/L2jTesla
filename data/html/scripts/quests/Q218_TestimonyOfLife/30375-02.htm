Priest Adonius:
Thank you so much, brother of the forest. We have run short of the ink and pens which are used for copying the sacred texts.
To make the ink, we need Spider Ichor. You can extract it from the spiders of the Cruma Marshlands. As for the pens, we create them with Harpy's Down. It would be most helpful if you could bring 20 of each. When you bring me these supplies I shall give you a copy of the book in return.
