Tablet of Vision:
Countless thoughts beyond comprehension sear painfully into your brain. The cascade of brief images is unintelligible at first...
As the pain subsides, a vision of the next tablet emerges, and it seems to be north of the border outpost. There must be a Resonance Amulet nearby! It looks like the legendary Naverius has it! And then the vision fades. You must immediately go north of the border outpost and find the Resonance Amulet.
