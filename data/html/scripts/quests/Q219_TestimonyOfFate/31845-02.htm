Bloody Pixy:
I know what you want! You have come for the bloody Fairy Dust! Do you think I would just give it to you for free?
If you do me a favor, I will give you what you seek. So listen closely!
Our queen is a collector of bones. She seems most happy when she is arranging the skulls of strange animals. I admit, she is a little eccentric, but she is my queen!
I will give you the Red Fairy Dust if you bring 10 skulls each, from the grandis, karul bugbear, Breka Orc Overlord, Leto Lizardman overlord! I swear on this jewel!
Grandis can be found in Death Pass. Karul bugbears can be found in Hunters Valley. Breka orc overlords are located in Breka's Stronghold. And, Leto Lizardman overlords can usually be found on the Plains of the Lizardmen.
