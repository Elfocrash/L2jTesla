Antharas Watchman Gilmore:
My personal aide wore this key around his neck. He stood by my side during the battle with Antharas 1,000 years ago. He was the first-born son in his family... This key was very important to him.
I believe one of his descendants is living in Giran Castle Town. He has the family name of Rodemai.
I am bound to this valley so cannot go myself. Would you please take the key to Rodemai in Giran Castle Town? I will pay you for your trouble.
