The journey of becoming a Wizard begins with the will of a magician to learn new things. Strong will to obtain knowledge is often compared to a flame. You must first find the token of flame.
In order to do that, you must meet the Flame Spirit. It is in the form of a lizard wrapped up in flames and is near a village damaged during the war, now called the Ruins of Despair. Look for it to the south of the village of Castle Gludio.
