Pilgrim of Darkness:
He offered to bring me the Divine Stone of Wisdom from the Ketra Orcs if I would give him what I had. Of course, if you bring me the stone first, you can have it! You can start a relationship with the Ketra Orcs if you go see Ketra's Messenger Wahkan in the Ketra Orc Outpost. If you're to get the stone, you must earn the trust of the Ketra Orcs. Remember to never help their mortal enemies, the Varka Silenos! You'd never regain their trust!
