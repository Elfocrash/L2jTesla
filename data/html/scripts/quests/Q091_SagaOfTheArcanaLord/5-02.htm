Tablet of Vision:
The Tablet of Vision becomes overwhelmed by bright lights. While you are holding the stone to work on it, you start to acquire a new sense of knowledge, with the voices of thousands of people reading scripture. You realize the truth of the world, yet understand nothing. One of the voices is a familiar female whisper. But it quickly disappears.
Slowly, you begin to visualize where the next Resonance Amulet is... in the valley of dust... you see a second Tablet of Vision... and a female warrior in platinum armor. She is holding the stone... She then unfolds her wings and flies away along with other female warriors......
In an instant, the images disappear and then the stone goes completely dark.
