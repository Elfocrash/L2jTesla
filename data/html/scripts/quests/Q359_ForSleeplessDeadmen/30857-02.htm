High Priest Orven:
Do you know about the Devastated Castle? It was built centuries ago to protect against the hordes of barbarians that were rampant even then. We were saved from the most recent invasion of the forces of Elmore by its mighty walls, except of course for the thousands of innocents killed when they poisoned our wells...
Centuries of violent conflict have left the castle in ruins and haunted by the ghosts of the multitudes of warriors who met their bloody end there.
