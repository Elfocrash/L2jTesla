GM commands page:123

ABNORMAL STATUTS

//chat_ban name - Mutes a player
//chat_unban name - Unmutes a player
//play_sounds music - Play a music from L2 music.
//earthquake Intensity, Duration - Creates an earthquake. Need both valors (1-20, duration in secs).
//para_all - Paralyzes all players.
//unpara_all - Unparalyze all players.
//para - Paralyze target
//unpara - Unparalyze target
//bighead - Gives target a big head.
//shrinkhead - Returns target's head to normal size.
//social social_id [radius|player] - Forces char/npc/s in target/self/player/radius to broadcast a social action.
//buff skillId skillLvl - Apply the given skill effect on target.
//effect skillID [level] - Forces char in target/self to broadcast a Magic Skill Use packet.
//atmosphere <ssqinfo dawn|dusk|red|regular> //atmosphere <sky day|night|red> - Sets current sky.
PLAYERS MANAGEMENT

//target name - Target a player (whenever he is).
//changelvl [0 to 10] - Change a targeted players access level.
//kick name - Disconnect a player.
//ban name - Kick and ban a player account.
//unban name - Unban a player account.
//show [number] - Opens list of online players [go to this page].
//account account - Show all players in one player's account.
//character part of name - Find a player by his name or a part of it (case-insensitive).
//dualbox number- find dualbox with "X" boxes.
//ip xxx.xxx.xxx.xxx- find characters logged under this IP.
//res - Resurrects either target, playername or radius
//heal [name|radius] - Fully heals CP/HP/MP of the selected character, or all characters in a given radius. Without target or invalid parameters, heal yourself.
//give_all_skills - Give to target all skills he can learn at his level with his class.
//show_skills - Lists targeted players skills.
//remove_skills - Removes the targeted players skills.
//skill_list - Opens skill menu.
//skill_index - Opens skill menu according to players stats.
//add_skill skillID level - Adds a skill to a targeted player.
//remove_skill skillID - Removes a skill from a targeted player.
//get_skills - Temporarily gives you the skills of a targeted player.
//reset_skills - Restores your skills to the way they were before the get_skills command.
//add_exp_sp_to_character - Opens the xp/sp menu of the targeted player.
//add_exp_sp number number - Adds xp/sp to targeted player.
//remove_exp_sp xp sp - Removes xp/sp to targeted player.
