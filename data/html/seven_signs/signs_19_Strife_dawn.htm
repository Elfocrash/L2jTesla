Priest of Dawn:
[ Seal of Strife ]
If we control the Seal of Strife, the rules of a castle siege will be changed as follows:
Strong Defensive Mercenaries:: The lord of each castle can employ strong defensive mercenaries whom they usually not allowed to hire.
Increased Castle Gate and Wall Defenses: The defensive power of the castle gates and walls are slightly increased.
Decreased Cost of Castle Gate and Wall Upgrades: The cost to upgrade the castle gates and walls is decreased.
Change Restart Village: If those who have no position die, they will revive in the 2nd nearest village, not the nearest one, when they restart.
Prohibit Siege Weapons: During a castle siege, the Dusk side will be unable to use siege weapons.
Additional Benefits:

Tax Rate Increases: The limit of the maximum tax rate that can be imposed by the lord of each castle is increased.
Change in CP: Combat Spirit Points (CP) of all Dawn members increases by 10%, while CP of all Dusk members decreases by 10%.
Disadvantages when the Seal is Stolen:

Wyvern Riding Prohibited: During the available period of the seal, the lord is not allowed to ride a Wyvern.
Cancel Defending Clan's Registration: During a castle siege, participation of all clans except for the clan that owns the castle is canceled.
