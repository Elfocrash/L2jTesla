Priest of Dawn:
[ Seal of Gnosis ]
When we control the Seal of Gnosis, we gain the following benefits:
Catacomb Possession: The Lords of Dawn will possess 6 catacombs, and non-members will be denied access to the catacombs. In the case of a draw, both groups will be able to enter.
Exclusive Trade with the Blacksmith of Mammon: We will be able to trade with the Blacksmith of Mammon, who appears in the catacombs. When we give Ancient Adena to the Blacksmith of Mammon, we will receive special services such as trading for C, D or some B grade equipment, or obtaining A grade weapons with special abilities.
Benefits of Teleport: By using Ancient Adena through the priest, we can teleport more cheaply in the Necropolis or the catacombs.
Orator of Revelations: An Orator of Revelation will appear in each village to grant our brethren the blessing of light.
Preacher of Doom: A Preacher of Doom will appear and curse our enemies, the Army of Dusk.
