Dusk Priestess:
[ Seal of Strife ]
If we control the Seal of Strife, the rules of a castle siege will be changed as follows:
Weak Defensive Mercenaries: The lord of each castle cannot employ general defensive mercenaries, and can only employ novice mercenaries that are weaker than general mercenaries.
Decreased Castle Gate and Wall Defenses: The defensive power of the castle gate and wall are decreased dramatically.
Increased Castle Gate and Wall Upgrade Costs: The cost to upgrade the castle and wall is increased dramatically.
Cancel Defending Clan's Registration: During a castle siege, participation of all clans except for the clan that ownes the castle is canceled.
Other benefits

Tax Rate Decrease: The limit of the maximum tax rate that can be imposed by the lord of each castle is decreased.
Change in CP: Combat Spirit Points (CP) of all Dusk members increases by 10%, and CP of all Dawn members decreases by 10%.
Wyvern Riding Prohibited: During the available period of the seal, Wyvern cannot be ridden.
Disadvantages When the Seal is Stolen:

Change Restart Village: If those who have no position die, they will revive in the 2nd nearest village, not the nearest one, when they restart.
Siege Weapons Prohibited: During a castle siege, siege weapons are not available for use.
