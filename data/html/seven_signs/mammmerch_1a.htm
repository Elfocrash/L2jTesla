Merchant of Mammon:
Ancient Adena is the currency that was used during the Elmoreden Empire. It was minted from gold of the highest purity, of a much higher quality than the coins used today. It is said that the emblem on the back of the coins possesses a mysterious power...
The Lords of Dawn and the Revolutionaries of Dusk are given bonuses in Ancient Adena. Sell them seal stones and they'll pay for them with Ancient Adena.
