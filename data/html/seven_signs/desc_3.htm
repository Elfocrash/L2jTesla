Priest of Dawn:
[ What is Seal Stone Collection? ]
1. Collecting seal stones and Seven Signs score: - Collecting seal stones is the most common method of earning points in the Seven Signs competition. The group that collects the most seal stones receives a higher score. - The score a group receives for collecting seal stones will count for 50% of the final score. The remaining 50% of the final score will be determined by the score the group earns during the Festival of Darkness.
2. Collecting seal stones: - To obtain seal stones you must kill Nephilm, Gigant, Lilim and Lith. - After you collect the seal stones, offer them to a Priest of Dawn. - Nephilim, Gigant, Lilim and Lith can only be found in necropolises or catacombs.
3. Collecting seal stones and claiming rewards: - During the Seven Signs competition, if the Lords of Dawn win, you will receive a reward for collecting seal stones. - The reward will be in proportion to the number of seal stones given to a priest during the competition, and is paid in the form of Ancient Adena. You can claim your reward by visiting a Priest of Dawn, located in each village.
