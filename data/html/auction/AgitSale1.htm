In order to put a clan hall up for auction, a deposit must be paid for the respective clan hall in the clan warehouse. The deposit is taken out of the clan warehouse at the moment the clan hall is put up for auction and is returned if the auction is completed normally.
If an auction is canceled, you cannot register an auction again for seven days and the deposit is not returned. The deposit also cannot be returned if the auction ends without any bids.
