Khavatari Toruku:
Tejakar Oroka! I am Toruku. I teach Kabart, the Orcs' martial art. Kabart is a martial art that trains your body to become a deadly weapon. Orcs that train in this do not depend on common weapons such as swords or spears, but learn combat skills that involve only swift movements and precise blows.
Pa'agrio of the flame teaches that we must continuously strive to strengthen ourselves to become stronger than all life which has breath. I have broken ties with the world and come to this isolated wasteland to fulfill these teachings and concentrate on the further training of Kabart.
