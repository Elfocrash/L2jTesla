Rizraell:
Next to the Elven Forest is the region of Oren where Humans live. There is also the Sea of Spores. However, there is a mountain range between the Elven Forest and Oren, so we can't pass freely.
The place called the Sea of Spores was once a battlefield of the great war between Elves and Men. Before that, it was a beautiful place, but the battle magic of the war polluted the ground, and the blight grows wider even to this very day. They say the Mystics at the Ivory Tower have set up force fields that are just barely sufficient to contain the blight. In the fight between us Elves and the Humans, nature was the ultimate loser.
