Sir Eric Rodemai:
Welcome to the Town of Aden. I am Eric Rodemai, a guard of the court. While Oren is the center of military affairs and Giran is the center of commerce, Aden is the center of politics, culture and religion.
This distinction should make Aden a bustling, metropolitan city, but it is not so. Its proximity to the border with Elmore creates an odd tension.
