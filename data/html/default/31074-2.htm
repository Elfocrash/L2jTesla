Corpse of Hutaku:
At first glance Zaken seems invincible, but perhaps I have spotted a chink in his armor. As dawn broke over the ragged remains of my party, I noticed that Zaken's recovery rate noticeably dropped.
Could it be that his awesome power is only available to him at night? If somehow I can survive the coming darkness, I might defeat him yet!
But, however I look at it, my chances are not good. I'm almost certainly done for!
Whoever reads this, avenge my death and the deaths of my comrades!
