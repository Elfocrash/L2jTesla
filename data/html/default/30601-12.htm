Newbie Guide:
The Newbie Travel Token is reserved for players of level 20 or below.
It allows the player to move to the start town of another tribe via the Newbie Guides in each town
Be cautionus, for in many cases, you may learn and practice certain skills and quests that are unique to the own of each tribe.
Once a player reaches level 20, he is no longer able to teleport using the Newbie Travel Token.
