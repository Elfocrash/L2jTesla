Ancestor Martankus:
I am Martankus of the Hestui. Although I've been dead for a thousand years and my body has long turned to ash, my soul remains and watches over my future generations!
This cave is the holy grave of the 7th tribe's heroes and also where young Orcs train their strength! I offer blessings and teachings to great offspring of the flame, but to the filthy invaders if other races, I will show no mercy!
