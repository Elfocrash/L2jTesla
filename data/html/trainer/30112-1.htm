Magister Maurius:
The evil that oppresses us is none other than the green dragon Antharas. It nests in the deepest part of the Dragon Valley. My brother Juris and I teach magic to our Elven brothers in the guild here, but we are also charged with the task of keeping watch on Antharas' movements.
If Antharas erupts again, the surrounding land will be covered with destruction and death. Therefore, we cannot be lazy about precautions.
