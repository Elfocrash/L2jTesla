Magister Hanellin:
The king of this country spent all of his time dealing with matters outside his kingdom, and had no idea what was happening deep within his own land.
Even now rivals eye this castle greedily. Elves speak of end times, but we Dark Elves aren't frightened at all. Humans are very interesting to us. They speak like Elves and act like Dark Elves. They are a fascinating race whose differences beguile us!
