Critique of Biel's Songs:
Legend says that the songs of Biel contained a magic power that could control an audience. The three types of magic in her songs are: seduction, sweet whispers and temptation.
In her time, she captivated her audience. It's said that Eva grew jealous, and created a flaw in each of her songs.
When a Song of Seduction is sung, the notes enter the listener's body and gradually make it heavier. The cure for this is the Song of Wind.
The Song of Whisper removes the listener's desire to attack. The Antidote for this is the Song of Water.
The Song of Temptation makes listeners defenseless. The Song of Vengeance removes the effect.
