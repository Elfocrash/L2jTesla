Khavatari Spirit Spellbook:
If you reach the limit while using the power of Ajirua, he'll materialize into a physical being and prevent you from ever using the fist of Khavatari!
Ajirua imparts a strong reflective ability in addition to Burning Fist, which fills your fist with the power of Pa'agrio, or the Soul Breaker, which can shatter a dead soul. When Ajirua awakes, he becomes an evil being. It's the duty of a grand Khavatari to slay him.
