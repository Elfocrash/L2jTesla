Guild Newsletter:
Chimera Golem: Created as a result of research conducted by hermits, the golem escaped the research center. Although a few years have passed, victims attacked by the golem are still being found. This article was written to prepare for an encounter.
(Omission) Unlike other golems, it can run very fast, but only temporarily. It has a sturdy body, but can be easily attacked when worked up due to the hate skill series. It's likely to be reckless when stimulated. It's best to avoid the golem entirely.
