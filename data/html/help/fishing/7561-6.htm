How to Fish:
The Fishing Shot was created by the Black Anvil guild at the request of Amfitley, the founder of the Fishermen's Guild. Basically, think of it as a soulshot for fishing.
The Fishing Shot enhances Reeling and Pumping. If these techniques are improperly used, the load on the fishing rod and the distance your fish can travel will double. So the Fishing Shot definitely could have some disadvantages.
However, if it's used correctly it can make up for any mistakes you make. Mastering Fishing Shot is the final stage to becoming an expert fisherman.
