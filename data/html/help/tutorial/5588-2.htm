Point of View (POV)
Moving the mouse while pressing down the right mouse button changes your character's point-of-view. This flexible change of viewpoint is especially useful during hunting, when your character has to look around for monsters.
Click the right mouse button again and the default POV will be restored.
Pressing Page Up/Page Down keys will also allow you to change your character's basic point-of-view.
